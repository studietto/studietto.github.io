Yuppy for iOS

As a good parent, you want your children to play and have fun, but learn and grow as well. And maybe relax a bit while they do so, right? Yuppy is a collection of educational mini-games that combine all those things.

TOP FEATURES

SUPER SAFE

Built with the safety of your little ones in mind. With zero ads in it, there is no risk of your children being exposed to inappropriate content. Suitable for lone play.

OFFLINE MODE

No network connection is required, so that you can play while travelling on a plane or subway, or from any remote part of your home.

SO MUCH CONTENT

Hundreds of coloring pages, puzzles, cards, objects, letters, numbers. There's so much content that getting bored is not an option!

APPROVED BY EDUCATORS

We worked with experienced teachers and educators to ensure that Yuppy is a great fit for the growth process of 3+ years old children.

PLAY AND LEARN TOGETHER

Take a look at Yuppy's educational games, designed for children aged 3+ years. Their professional and artistic illustrations and fun sound effects make it very engaging, compelling and satisfying to play, while being really useful for your kids' growth and learning process.

It's great for both lone play or with parents. You decide if you want to have fun together or maybe give your children some solo time while you finally relax a bit with a well-deserved peace of mind!

COLORING BOOK

Plenty of outline drawings, in different styles and subjects, to fill with color. Choose from a lot of awesome colors and tools such as crayons, pens, pencils, brushes, highlighters and more. We promise you not only your children are going to love it, but you will as well!

SHAPE MATCHING GAME

Match a huge selection (350+) of objects and animals with their shape. This game is a good brain teaser that helps to develop abstract and spatial thinking.

A WORLD OF PUZZLES

70+ puzzles, all available in 2x2 and 3x3 mode, for different skill levels. A shadow in background can optionally be displayed as an aid if your kid is just starting out or gets stuck, so it's really enjoyable for a wide range of ages.

62 LETTERS AND NUMBERS TO TRACE

Help your children learn the basics of writing by having them trace all letters (both uppercase and lowercase) and numbers with their finger. Don't worry, the tracing logic has a good deal of tolerance so that it's encouraging and satisfying.

MEMORY CARD GAME

The classic memory card game, with 10+ different decks with different characters and objects to choose from. This game helps preschoolers practice their focus, memory, and matching skills. You can play with 8, 12, 16 or 20 cards, so that everyone at every level can have fun and grow with it.

ANIMAL SOUNDS

This interactive and immersive environment allows your children to discover the realistic sounds and names of over 60 animals! Discover & learn the real sounds of farm, wild and exotic animals from all over the world. Listen to animals in their natural environments; in the woods, Africa, Australia, Asia, in the ocean and more!
